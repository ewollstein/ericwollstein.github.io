Each player picks a hero at the start of the game. The various heroes have their own strengths & weaknesses within their deck, as well as having an innate power to use. Some act in a protective way by taking damage for their allies, some rely on manipulating the various decks on the table, while others use their abilities to dish out the damage. There are cards that heal or do damage to multiple targets at once, so the math can get a little tricky. In the case of healing it's safe to assume there will usually be at least three hero targets to heal. When it comes to damage there will be times when there is only the supervillain left to attack, or there may be many targets to choose from, it will all depend on the Environment and Villain chosen and the tactics your team employs. We can't say for sure from one turn to another, but we will assume it averages out to three targets available at any given time.
